    The URL-generator

    Save settings without cookies

    • You can save your Ixquick preferences by clicking on the "Save Setting" button on the "Settings" page.
    • This will save your settings on your computer in a 'cookie'- a small piece of data stored on your hard drive - called 'preferences'.
    • Ixquick's 'preferences' cookie is a non-identifiable cookie and safe to accept, but cookies in general can pose a threat to your privacy.
    • People who prefer not to accept cookies or delete them regularly would not be able to keep their preferences this way.
    • For those people, Ixquick has especially provided the "Generate URL" button at the bottom of the "Settings" page.
    • Pushing that button a specific Ixquick URL containing your selected settings will be generated.
    • It can be generated in readable or obfuscated form.
    • The generated URL can now be be directly set as your homepage or copied and used as a bookmark.
    • Using this URL all of your preferences will be used the next time you visit Ixquick.
    • For even more convenience you can hit 'Install Custom Plugin' after you created your URL.
    • This will install a search bar plugin that includes your settings.
    • You may need to delete previously saved versions first from the search bar's drop down list.
    • Make sure the 'Keyword' you enter differs from the keyword you may have saved before.
