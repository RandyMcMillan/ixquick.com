    Ixquick ツールバーのダウンロード

    Add the Ixquick Toolbar to your browser today. It's fun and easy to use.

    Better Privacy:
    • Direct access to Ixquick's privacy search. No Snooping. No Storage. No Tracking.
    • Deletes 3rd party tracking cookies you may have picked up during browsing instantly.
    • Even deletes the “Flash cookies and/ or Supercookies” from your computer.
    • Uses HTTPS to prevent ISP's from snooping through your personal data.
    More features:
    • Click on the arrow next to Ixquick's logo to customize, your toolbar search, delete cookies or change the "setting" options.
    • Easy switching between Ixquick and sister website Startpage.com. (Meta-search vs. Google search - both full Privacy)
    • Use to search through any website quickly.
    • Highlight your search terms on the website with.
    • Translate text with some outside help.
    • The Zoom in or out buttons allow you to enlarge or reduce any webpage to make it more readable.

    The Toolbar has various hover-over explanations and instructions. Hover over the icons to get more information on a specific topic. Some frequently asked questions can be found here.

    By downloading the Ixquick Toolbar, you agree to our Terms and Conditions.
    でのインストール
    Click here to install the Toolbar from the Chrome Web Store

    Download

    手順 (問題が発生しない場合)
    Google Chrome Installations:
    • Confirm that you accept the Ixquick Toolbar terms and conditions.
    • Click Download.
    • Google Chrome Installations:
    • In Chrome, open Preferences - Extensions, or type chrome://extensions into the URL bar.
    • Drag and drop the extension file you downloaded into Chrome's list of extensions. The Ixquick extension filename will end in .crx.
    • Click 'Add' to confirm installation.
    システム要件:
    • Internet Explorer 8.x (Windows XP)
    • Internet Explorer 9.x (Windows XP/7)
    • Firefox 12 (All platforms)
    • Safari 5.0 through 5.1.5 (Mac OS X)
    • Opera 15 and higher (All platforms)
