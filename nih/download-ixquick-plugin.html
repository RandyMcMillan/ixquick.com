    You can also use Ixquick as the default search engine within the address bar of the Safari browser. To do so, please install our browser extension by following the steps below. No other browser settings will be changed.


    1. Enable extensions in your Safari browser. To do so, click on the word 'Safari' in the Mac OS menu bar (next to the Apple icon). From the menu that appears, select Preferences, Extensions, and then change the On/Off switch to On. You may then close the Preferences display.
    2. Click on the link below to download the extension.
      Ixquick Safari Extension
    3. Double-click the file 'Ixquick-HTTPS.safariextz' from Safari downloads menu and click 'Open' if prompted.
    4. A Safari browser prompt will open asking you whether you want to install it or not. Click the "Install" button to complete the process.

    Now you are ready to perform Ixquick searches from your Safari Search bar. Enjoy!

    You can now easily add Ixquick to your Konqueror browser.
    To add Ixquick as web shortcut to your konqueror browser follow below Instructions.
    Instructions:

    1. Goto "Settings" in the Konqueror menu of your Browser.
    2. Then Select "configure konqueror..".
    3. Then choose "Enhanced Browsing or Web Shortcuts" from the sidebar on the left, and you will get a nice long list of shortcut.
    4. Click "New" button.
    5. Enter 'Ixquick' in Search Provider "Name:"
    6. Enter 'https://ixquick.com/do/metasearch.pl?query=\{@}' in "Search URI:".
    7. Enter your own shortcuts e.g: 'ix,ixq,ixquick' in "URI Shortcuts:".
    8. Click OK.


    After following these instructions, you're ready to search: just enter "ix: Web Intensive" and press Enter!
